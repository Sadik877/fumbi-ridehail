Privacy Policy

Last updated July 2026

1. What we collect

When you create an account, book a ride or delivery, or apply to drive, we collect information you provide directly — name, email, phone number, city, and, for drivers, license and vehicle details. We also collect trip data (pickup/drop-off locations, timestamps, fares) and device/usage data needed to operate the service securely.

2. How we use it

We use your data to match riders with drivers, calculate fares, process payments, verify driver identity and vehicle documents, send trip and account notifications, and improve platform safety and reliability.

3. Location data

Pickup and drop-off addresses are geocoded via OpenStreetMap to calculate distance, ETA, and fare. Live location is shared with your matched driver or passenger only for the duration of an active trip.

4. Payment data

Card payments are processed by Flutterwave or Paystack directly — MoveX does not store your full card number. Wallet balances and transaction references are stored to maintain an accurate ledger of your account.

5. Sharing

We share the minimum information needed for a trip to happen (e.g. your name and pickup location with your matched driver). We do not sell personal data to third parties.

6. Your rights

You can access, correct, or request deletion of your account data from your Profile page, or by contacting support. Some trip and payment records are retained for a limited period to meet legal and safety obligations.

7. Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to privacy@movex.app or via our Contact page.

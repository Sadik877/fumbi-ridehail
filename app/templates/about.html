Our story

Building the transport layer for African cities.

Fumbi started in 2021 with a simple observation: the biggest ride-hailing apps were built for cities they'd never driven in. Unnamed roads, informal addresses, and cash-first economies needed a platform designed around them — not retrofitted for them. Today we operate across 18 cities, connecting riders and senders with drivers who know their streets better than any algorithm.
